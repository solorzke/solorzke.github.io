Small Business Websites

Static & Dynamic Websites for Small Businesses

I provided consultancy to small businesses that were interested in building a website for their business to bring in more customers and generate more sales. The common goal was mostly to present the identity of their business to the web and explain what their services are and how to contact them. What's important is making sure the site gets to the point of what their store is about, and why you should pay them a visit.

Due to the simplicity of their requests, the best way to build was from scratch with HTML/CSS/JavaScript and a library like Bootstrap to simplify its responsive design. I also experimented with React JS for the first time in one of these projects to get more familiar with the library.
