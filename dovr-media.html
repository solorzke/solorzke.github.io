Dovr Media

Official Company Site for Dovr Media

One of the first projects during my time working at Dovr Media was to revamp their old website and modernize it with a better, expansive site that presented who we are and what our services are to prospective clients. We target clients who have small businesses that want to have an online presence in advertising, E-Commerce, and opening multiple sales channels in online shopping with Shopify. Site was built using Bootstrap.

How to complete this?

We collected the assets (graphics, color schemes, documentation/links) from the old site and designed new UI templates that rivaled our competitor's websites while retaining our messaging as simple but objective so that our clients both knew how to navigate the site and understanding what we offer.

  • Collect old assets

    Including messaging, graphics, third-party applications, and links.

  • Design & Sketching

    Create new graphics for new layout structure of pages such as landing page, services, team members, results, and contact us.

  • Launch

    Combine assets together and build the site in HTML or Bootstrap
