Project

Recipe Search - Mobile Application

Github Android App: @solorzke

 |

Github Scripts: @solorzke

The purpose was to develop an application that would serve as the mobile interface of the mentoring program that the program founders wanted to establish at NJIT. The application required an NJIT Authentication system that allowed users with a credible UCID/Password to use. Mentors are able to schedule appointment/meeting check-ins throughout the semester to track the student's progress, evaluate their critical goals to achieve during the semester (set forth by the mentor) and deliver insightful resources provided by the mentor through the application's resource pipeline.

I was the lead developer in the development process and designed the architecture of the application. Collaborating with a team of 4, I worked in managing our front-end, back-end, production and deployment servers, writing MySQL/PHP scripts to migrate the exchange of data, designing the front-end interface in XML, and class architecture through Java, using REST API to create our network requests, and setting up Firebase's Messaging service for our instant messaging service/notifications for our users.

I also ran testing of the application to identify design flaws, bugs, and conduct focus groups to receive feedback about the design. Our workflow was conducted under SCRUM to deliver a working prototype in a short deadline before continuing to finalize the application for the next year.
